    Support & Getting Started

    New to prosthetics? Start here for an overview and help using the Ace Prosthetics Hub.

    Getting Started

    Choosing and using a prosthetic can feel overwhelming. This hub is designed to help you:

    • Tutorials - Use the Tutorials section to learn about prosthetics and how to use them.
    • Prosthetics - Use the Prosthethetic Directory to find and filter through the extensive list of prosthetics.
    • Connect - Join the Community Forum to share experiences and ask questions.
    • Contact Us - Need direct help? Visit our Contact page to get in touch with support.

    Using this website

    • Navigation — Use the menu at the top. The Tutorials item opens a dropdown when you click it. On mobile, tap the menu icon to open the full navigation.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the Ace Prosthetics Hub?

    The Ace Prosthetics Hub is a dedicated online community and resource center. We provide accessible video tutorials, a comprehensive directory of global and local (Philippines) prosthetic providers, and a community forum where individuals can share experiences, ask questions, and support one another.

    Do I need to create an account to use the website?

    No, you do not need an account to access our public resources! Anyone can browse the video tutorials, read the prosthetics directory, and view public forum posts. However, you will need to Sign Up for a free account if you want to post your own questions in the Community Forum or reply to other members.

    How do I reset my password if I forget it?

    If you are having trouble logging in, click the "Sign In" button at the top of the page and select the "Forgot Password" link. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we will send you a secure link to reset your password.

    How do I find a prosthetic clinic or manufacturer near me?

    Navigate to our Prosthetics page using the main menu. There, you will find our comprehensive directory table. It is divided into Global Manufacturers and regional providers (specifically in the Philippines). You can review their specialties, the types of services they offer, and click directly to their websites.

    The video tutorial is playing, but I cannot hear it well. Are there subtitles?

    Yes! Accessibility is a top priority for us. All of our embedded video tutorials have Closed Captions (CC) enabled by default. If they do not appear, simply click the "CC" button in the bottom corner of the video player. Additionally, a full written transcript is provided just below the video player in the tutorial details.

    Can I suggest a new clinic, NGO, or tutorial to be added to the site?

    Absolutely. We are always looking to expand our resources. Please head over to the Contact Us page and send us a message with the details of the organization or video you would like to see added. Our admin team will review it!

    How do I ask a question in the Community Forum?

    First, ensure you are logged into your account. Navigate to the Community Forum tab, and click the "New Post" or "Ask a Question" button. Give your post a clear title and provide as much detail as possible in the body so other community members can give you the best advice.

    Are the forums moderated?

    Yes. To ensure the Ace Prosthetics Hub remains a safe, respectful, and helpful environment, our admin team monitors forum activity. Hate speech, harassment, spam, and dangerous medical misinformation are strictly prohibited and will be removed.

    I am experiencing a technical issue with the website (e.g., a video won't load, or a link is broken). What should I do?

    We apologize for the inconvenience! Please visit our Contact Us page and fill out the form, or reach out directly to our Technical Support email listed on that page. Please include a brief description of the issue and what device/browser you are using so we can fix it right away.

    Is my personal information safe?

    Yes. We use industry-standard secure databases (Supabase) to handle all user accounts and authentication. We will never sell your personal email or contact information to third parties.
